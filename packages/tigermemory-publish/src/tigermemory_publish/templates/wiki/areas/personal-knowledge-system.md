# Personal Knowledge System

## Summary

Areas are long-running responsibilities. Use this page to describe the knowledge
system you want to keep healthy over months or years.

## What Belongs Here

- Long-term responsibilities.
- Habits and recurring reviews.
- Personal operating principles.
- Ongoing learning areas.

## Current Standard

Stable knowledge should live in Markdown. Short-lived notes can stay in local
memory until they become durable enough for the Wiki.

## Review Questions

- Which pages are still useful?
- Which notes are outdated?
- Which repeated questions deserve a clearer Wiki page?
